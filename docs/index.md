## Overview

SportsLabResearch Blood Pressure Analyzer is an open scientific software platform developed for the automated analysis of resting blood pressure according to the recommendations of the European Society of Hypertension (ESH 2023).

The software imports blood pressure measurements, performs automatic quality control, calculates the selected baseline period and generates professional clinical reports.

## Scientific Framework

The software follows the recommendations of the European Society of Hypertension (ESH 2023).

Current home blood pressure threshold:

- **135 mmHg** systolic
- **85 mmHg** diastolic
